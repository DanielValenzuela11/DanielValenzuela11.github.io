Fake Basketball Quotes

Continuing on with the basketball theme, another great source of basketball related laughter can be found on a twitter page, with the sole purpose of creating fake quotes said by players. Most people who come across the posts will probably know that they are fake based on the context, however, as the posts get shared and retweeted, it becomes harder for people to realize that the posts are not real, mainly due to not recognizing the original source of the posts. Eventually, this leads to reputable sources and major sports news outlets talking about the false quotes on their shows, under the impression that the quotes are actually legitimate. Although the dead giveaway that the quote is not real is from looking at the actual name of the twitter profile, which I will leave for you to see.
